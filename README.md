# Lock — Client Background & North Star Metrics

## Client Background
Lock is a South Korea–based e-commerce storefront specialising in smart locks and home access products for residential and small business customers.

As competition in smart home hardware grows and more customers buy online, Lock needs a clear picture of what’s actually driving purchases across their site.

This analysis will show which products and regions perform best, where shoppers drop off, and which fixes are most likely to lift conversion and revenue.

## North Star Metrics
- **Product performance:** Measure revenue, units sold, and AOV to get a clear read on overall sales performance.  
- **Regional results:** Analyse product performance by region to identify your strongest markets and focus marketing where it drives the most impact.  
- **Website interaction analysis:** Analyse funnel conversion rates and user behaviour to pinpoint drop-offs and prioritise UX improvements.
